    About Us

    Founded in 2021 in the heart of Kyiv, Pokupka Games is a burgeoning game development company that has quickly made a name for itself in the gaming industry. Our team, a blend of seasoned veterans and fresh talent, brings together a wealth of experience and a fresh perspective to every project we undertake.

    At Pokupka Games, we’re passionate about creating games that are not only entertaining but also immersive and innovative. Our mission is to push the boundaries of what’s possible in gaming, combining cutting-edge technology with creative storytelling to deliver unique gaming experiences.

    We pride ourselves on our collaborative culture, where every team member’s input is valued, and creativity is nurtured. Based in Kyiv, a city known for its rich history and vibrant tech scene, we draw inspiration from our surroundings to infuse our games with unique flavors and perspectives.

    Since our inception, we’ve been committed to diversity and inclusivity, both in our games and within our team. We believe that a diverse team leads to more innovative ideas and perspectives, which is vital in creating games that resonate with a wide range of players.

    As we continue to grow and evolve, our focus remains on developing high-quality games that players around the world will love and remember. Join us on our exciting journey as we explore new horizons in the realm of gaming.
